SDG Push
Diagnostic

The Diagnostic gives a rapid, integrated overview of national SDG progress.

It’s data-driven design unlocks SDG interlinkages – areas where concerted policy investment can spur tangible progress across multiple fronts.
It also powers UNDP’s 90+ 'Integrated SDG Insights Reports,' which were launched to support countries ahead of the 2023 SDG Summit.



Each SDG Insights Report leverages data, AI and systems intelligence through four key features:

1

SDG Moment

Assesses national growth trajectories, focusing on environmental sustainability and inclusiveness, using data from IMF-WEO, World Bank, and Global Carbon Budget.


2

Trends & Priorities

Track SDG progress using official UN statistics and machine learning to analyze national policy documents and align them with the SDGs.


3

SDG Interlinkages

Map synergies and trade-offs among SDG targets through global literature reviews, identifying policy pathways with the highest multiplier effects.


4

Finance & Stimulus

Provides insights into fiscal and financial stress indicators, offering options for stimulus and other financing strategies to accelerate SDG progress.
